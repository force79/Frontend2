About Me

Hii This is Ankit Kumar Sharma, I'm a BCA 1st Year Student at Shri Ramswroop Memorial University. I created this website because many students were struggling with logging in repeatedly and entering OTPs. Some students didn’t even have access to their registered phone numbers, which made the process even harder.

To make the process easier, I built this attendance tool to fetch reports directly without all the extra steps. You can learn more about how it works on the Information Page.
