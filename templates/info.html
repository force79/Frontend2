How This Tool Works

This tool is built in Python to automate the university’s official website. Since I don’t have access to the university’s database, the tool always live-checks your attendance directly from the portal.

Every time you use this tool, it securely logs in with the details you provide and downloads your official attendance report in real-time. Nothing is stored or reused, so your data stays private.

Simply enter your Roll Number, Password, and Term (for example: 2501) to get your latest report.
